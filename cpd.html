Using Crystals with Reflexology

Val also runs CPD (Continued Professional Development) Courses for Reflexologists and Trainee Reflexologists. If you take the one day course, you will be awarded with 12 CPD points.

The CPD Course covers the following topics:

  • Choosing and Cleansing Crystals
  • Using Crystals in the Treatment Room
  • Learning about Grounding Crystals
  • The Crystal Pyramid
  • Chakra Points on the body and the feet and using crystals on the feet
  • Crystal Wands - Using these during a Reflexology session

Course Details
